Welcome to ExoList

ExoList is a community-driven leaderboard for ExoRacer. Our goal is to track the hardest levels and the best players.

Verification Rules

  • Uploaded levels will not appear until they have a first victor.
  • The first victor becomes the verifier of the level.
  • Levels are assigned a difficulty rating by the community.
  • Ping @nexuszenplays to add the levels to a list/suggestions.
